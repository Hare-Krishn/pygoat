A09 – Security Logging & Alerting Failures ( OWASP Top 10 2025 )

Security Logging & Alerting Failures occur when an application cannot adequately detect, escalate, or respond to security-relevant events. Without effective logging, monitoring, and alerting, attacks and breaches may remain undetected, significantly increasing their impact.

  • Security events such as logins, access control failures, and high-value actions are not logged or are logged inconsistently
  • Warnings, errors, and exceptional conditions generate insufficient or unclear log data
  • Logs are not monitored for suspicious behavior or are stored without integrity protection
  • Alerting thresholds, escalation paths, or response playbooks are missing or ineffective
  • Active attacks do not trigger real-time or near real-time alerts
  • Penetration testing or automated scans fail to generate alerts
